About Dublin Street Parking

Data sources Parking data is sourced from data.smartdublin.ie and the open data portals of Dublin City Council, Fingal County Council, South Dublin County Council, and Dún Laoghaire-Rathdown County Council. Data is provided for informational purposes and may not reflect recent changes to on-street signage or regulations.
