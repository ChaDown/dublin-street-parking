Parking Rules & Permits — Tallaght

Pay & Display parking In Tallaght, pay & display parking is managed by South Dublin County Council. Purchase tickets from roadside machines or use the Payzone app. Always check signs — hours and tariffs vary by street.
Permit zones Some streets in Tallaght operate permit parking schemes. These are indicated by yellow lines and signs. Non-permit holders may park for a limited period during restricted hours — check the local signage carefully.
