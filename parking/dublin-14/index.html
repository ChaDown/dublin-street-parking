Parking Rules & Permits — Dublin 14

Pay & Display parking In Dublin 14, pay & display parking is managed by South Dublin County Council. Purchase tickets from roadside machines or use the Payzone app. Always check signs — hours and tariffs vary by street.
Permit zones Some streets in Dublin 14 operate permit parking schemes. These are indicated by yellow lines and signs. Non-permit holders may park for a limited period during restricted hours — check the local signage carefully.
